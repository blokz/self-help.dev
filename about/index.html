About us

Who?

Currently the only developer working on this project is @sn0n.

What?

self-help.dev is (heavily under construction) and a project of blokz.io aimed at helping people help themselves.

When?

Started this site on Jun 20, 2020. With a full launch of July 1, 2020 (expected, under construction).

Where?

This project was brought on by a desire to have a web portal of sorts for things that mattered to me, not what other's felt I should have in my quicklinks or whatever else, and thus this profile project was begun.. and as with all web projects, has grown over time..

Why?

A selfish need to be happy and leave something behind for others to use and hopefully get some assistance from in some aspect of their lives. I suppose one could also say to get riches,.. but thats not really the intent, We're good with just enough to get by.

How?

By the 'magic' of HTML, CSS, some Javascript and a few libraries.
Namely Hive.JS, Nanogen, and Material Design Lite.
